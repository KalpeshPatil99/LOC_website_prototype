Domains



Machine Learning

Machine learning is a type of artificial intelligence (AI) that provides computers with the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed. Machine learning focuses on the development of computer programs that can change when exposed to new data.

Computer Vision

Computer vision is concerned with the automatic extraction, analysis and understanding of useful information from a single image or a sequence of images. It involves the development of a theoretical and algorithmic basis to achieve automatic visual understanding.

Block Chain

It’s the technology that underpins digital currencies and ensures that all transactions are properly conducted and recorded. Blockchain technologies make tracking and managing digital identities both secure and efficient resulting in seamless sign-on and reduced fraud.

Big Data

Big data analytics is the process of examining large and varied data sets -- i.e., big data -- to uncover hidden patterns, unknown correlations, market trends, customer preferences and other useful information that can help organizations make more-informed business decisions.

Networking And Security

Network security is a specialized field in computer networking that involves securing a computer network infrastructure. Explore the world of Networking Security and build an innovative product around it, or find any unsolved/unknown bug. Remember ETERNALBLUE?

Rules and regulations!

Teams can be anywhere from two to four members. To ensure a level field for all contestants, all code, design and assets must be created during the duration of the Hackathon. You are, however, free to make plans, create wireframes, and brainstorm prior to the event. You are allowed to use 3rd party services, APIs, open source projects, libraries, and frameworks.
There will be no alcohol, smoking allowed at the event. Behave professionally.
Remember that harassment and racist, sexist, or exclusionary jokes are not appropriate for this event*. Attendees violating these rules may be asked to leave the Hackathon at the sole discretion of the Hackathon organizers. Thank you for helping make this a welcoming, friendly event for all.
